Tutorial to participte in Challenge of EvalAI


Step 1: First of all you need to have account of EvalAI, if you dont have click the link : https://evalai.cloudcv.org/auth/signup



Step 2 : After you've created account verify your e-mail address following screen will appear...



Step 3 : Login to you account. https://evalai.cloudcv.org/auth/login



Step 4 : You'll be redirected to dashboard where you'll see following screen...



Step 5 : Now You've to Click on Ongoing Chllenges.



Step 6 : Now Ongoing Challenges will appear, choose your desired one.



Step 7 : Now see overview and then click on participate option.



Afterwards create a new team and then select create participant team, then select your team at the right side option and click on participate.



You've Successfully Participated in Your Desired Challenge...
